About

Faulhaber Design Ltd is a landscape architecture and planning consultancy based in Devon. We bring considered landscapes from concept to consent — combining photorealistic 3D rendering in Blender and Photoshop with GPS-aligned ordnance survey mapping that grounds every proposal in its real landscape. We guide projects through design, visualisation, and planning permission with precision and care.

Services

Landscape Architecture

Masterplanning, detailed design, and hard and soft landscaping across residential, commercial, and public realm projects.

Planning & Permissions

Expert navigation of the planning process — from pre-application advice through to consent, appeals, and condition discharge.

3D Visualisation

Photorealistic rendering in Blender and Photoshop to communicate proposals clearly to clients, planners, and the public.

GPS & Ordnance Mapping

GPS-aligned survey mapping that overlays design proposals onto real-world viewpoints for accurate, grounded presentations.
