About

Faulhaber Design Ltd is a landscape architecture and planning consultancy based in Devon. We bring considered landscapes from concept to consent — combining photorealistic 3D rendering in Blender and Photoshop with GPS-aligned ordnance survey mapping that grounds every proposal in its real landscape. We guide projects through design, visualisation, and planning permission with precision and care.
