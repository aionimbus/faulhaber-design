About

Faulhaber Design Ltd is a visualisation consultancy based in the UK, established in 1999 by Klaus Faulhaber, a Chartered Landscape Architect with photomontage expertise dating to the early 1990s.

We provide a comprehensive photomontage service to Landscape Architects, Planners, Architects and Developers. All our work is carried out to the exacting current guidelines published by the Landscape Institute and Institute of Environmental Management & Assessment — as well as producing photomontages to comply with Scottish Natural Heritage, the London Views Management Framework and The Highland Council Visualisation Standards.

Services

Photomontage

We prepare accurate photomontages for planning applications and appeals, using industry standard and cutting edge software. With many years of experience we deliver images tailored to our clients' needs.

Zones of Theoretical Visibility

Using OS data and LiDAR mapping combined with bespoke GIS plug-ins, we provide comprehensive ZTV analysis for development planning and LVIA work.

Photography

We provide the photography necessary for any photomontage project. All photography is carried out to the current Landscape Institute Guidelines.

2D and 3D CAD

Using a combination of software packages we provide a comprehensive service for small to very large scale projects.
